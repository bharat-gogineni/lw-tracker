Hero Tracker

Hero EXP uses the built-in per-level table (to 175) plus an optional rate for higher levels. Hero Shards use the fixed 0-5 star tier table, while Weapon Shards use the fixed 0-30 requirement table. Owned EXP and shards drive Ready the same way.

EXP needed sums the in-game table for each level step (and your optional rate past 175). Max HQ is the highest HQ band required on that level path. Hero stars use five tiers per star: 0-1 star costs 5 each, 1-2 costs 10, 2-3 costs 20, 3-4 costs 60, and 4-5 costs 100. Use 5 star / 0 tier for max star. Hover a cell for still-short hints. Ready means all three shortfalls are zero.
